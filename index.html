TIPPING DUMPER

A Forward Tipping Dumper (FTD) is commonly used on construction sites. It is used to transport large quantities of materials across a site, usually on rough, undulating terrain. Its ability to handle a mixture of terrain and carry large loads makes it a flexible piece of equipment. This course will equip you with the knowledge, skills and confidence to operate the machine safely, protecting both you and those around you. This course offers two certificate options: operating either a tracked or wheeled machine depending on your needs.
